Video Editing & Motion Graphics

Video editing and motion graphics are essential tools for storytelling in the digital age. I specialize in creating engaging video content that captures attention and communicates your message effectively. Whether you need a promotional video, social media content, or an explainer-style motion graphics piece, I can help you bring your vision to life. I also create branded motion graphics video assets such as lower-thirds, intros and outros, and onscreen text.

My Process

I follow a structured process to create effective video content:

  1. Pre-production: This phase involves brainstorming ideas, creating a script or storyboard, and planning the overall vision for the video.
  2. Production: During production, I capture all necessary footage, whether it's through provided assets, animation, or a combination of both.
  3. Post-production: This is where the magic happens! I edit the footage, add graphics and effects, and ensure the final product aligns with your vision.
  4. Delivery: I provide the final infographic in the required format for your use.

Pricing

My pricing for video editing & motion graphics varies based on the complexity and scope of the project. I offer competitive rates and can provide a detailed quote after discussing your specific needs. Please contact me for more information.
