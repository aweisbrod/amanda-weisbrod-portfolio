    Branding

    Branding is essential for establishing a strong identity and connection with your audience. I specialize in creating cohesive branding solutions that reflect your values and vision through style guides, logos, icons, presentations, one sheets, and website design, along with other visual collateral.

    My Process

    I follow a structured process to create effective branding collateral:

    1. Research: I begin by understanding your brand, target audience, and the message you want to convey.
    2. Concept Development: Next, I create design concepts and sketches that align with your vision and goals.
    3. Design: After reviewing the prototypes, I create the visual elements of your brand, including logos, color palettes, and typography.
    4. Revisions: I value your feedback and make necessary revisions to ensure the final branding collateral meets your expectations.
    5. Delivery: I provide the final branding assets in the required format for your use.

    Pricing

    My pricing for branding packages varies based on the complexity and scope of the project. I offer competitive rates and can provide a detailed quote after discussing your specific needs. Please contact me for more information.
