Writing

Communicating a narrative successfully takes advanced writing skills and a penchant for storytelling, which I am proud to possess. I specialize in writing news stories, feature articles, copy and scripts for media outlets and non-profits.

My Process

My process depends on the type of material being written, but I generally follow these steps:

  1. Consultation: I begin by discussing your project goals, target audience, and key messages to ensure a clear understanding of your needs.
  2. Research: I conduct thorough research on the topic, gathering relevant information through interviews and existing literature to support the narrative.
  3. Outline: I create a detailed outline to organize the structure and flow of the article or copy.
  4. Writing: I craft engaging and informative content that resonates with the target audience.
  5. Editing: I review and refine the content for clarity, coherence, grammar, and style to ensure high-quality writing.
  6. Feedback & Revisions: I welcome your input and make necessary revisions to ensure the final content meets your expectations.
  7. Final Delivery: I deliver the polished article or copy in your preferred format, ready for publication or use.

Pricing

My pricing for articles and copywriting varies based on the complexity and scope of the project. I offer competitive rates and can provide a detailed quote after discussing your specific needs. Please contact me for more information.
